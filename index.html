Avianca LifeMiles: Star Alliance Awards & Credit Cards

LifeMiles is the frequent-flyer program of Avianca and a popular way to book Star Alliance flights. This guide explains how LifeMiles are earned, how redemptions and promos work, what elite tiers look like, and how credit cards and bank transfers can rapidly grow your balance.

Best Cards for Earning LifeMiles

1. What the LifeMiles Program Is

LifeMiles is Avianca’s loyalty program and a full Star Alliance frequent-flyer scheme. It is known for frequent mileage sales, one-way awards and the ability to mix cabins on a single itinerary.

The core components are:

  • LifeMiles – miles you earn and redeem for flights and upgrades.
  • Elite status tiers – with benefits on Avianca and Star Alliance partners.
  • Bank and card integrations – multiple banks allow transfers into LifeMiles.

2. How LifeMiles Are Earned

You can earn LifeMiles via:

  • Avianca flights – miles based on booking class and distance or revenue (depending on fare rules).
  • Star Alliance partner flights – credit eligible flights to LifeMiles instead of other programs.
  • Co-branded credit cards in certain markets.
  • Bank point transfers – from selected bank rewards ecosystems.
  • Regular mileage sales and promos – LifeMiles frequently sells miles at a discount.

Many LifeMiles users buy or transfer miles strategically during promotions and then redeem them for premium-cabin flights they would never pay cash for.

3. How Credit Cards Boost LifeMiles Earnings

Credit cards help fill your LifeMiles account even when you are not flying. Depending on your country, you may have:

  • Direct LifeMiles co-branded cards that earn miles on every purchase.
  • Bank rewards cards whose points convert into LifeMiles at set ratios.
  • Welcome bonuses that can cover one or more long-haul award tickets.
  • Promotional transfer bonuses where banks offer extra miles when you move points into LifeMiles.

A common strategy is to hold flexible bank points first, then move them into LifeMiles when transfer bonuses align with the award you want to book.

4. Redemption Options in LifeMiles

LifeMiles can be redeemed for:

  • Avianca flights across the Americas and beyond.
  • Star Alliance partner flights including long-haul business and first class.
  • Mixed-cabin itineraries combining economy and business segments on the same ticket.
  • One-way awards for flexibility.
  • Occasional non-flight redemptions depending on region and promotions.

LifeMiles is particularly popular among enthusiasts for premium-cabin Star Alliance redemptions booked with purchased or transferred miles.

5. LifeMiles Elite Status Tiers

LifeMiles elite status offers benefits on Avianca and Star Alliance partners, such as priority services and additional baggage. While names and metrics can change, the structure typically includes:

  • LifeMiles – base member tier.
  • Elite Silver/Gold – mid-tiers with incremental perks.
  • Top-tier Premier status – highest recognition with extensive Star Alliance benefits.

Credit cards themselves usually do not grant full alliance-wide status in LifeMiles, but they can help you fly more often and in higher cabins, making qualification easier.
